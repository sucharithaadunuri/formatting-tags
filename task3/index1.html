REBEL STAR PRABHAS


Uppalapati Venkata Suryanarayana Prabhas Raju ([pɾabʱaːs] born 23 October 1979), known mononymously as Prabhas, is an Indian actor who works predominantly in Telugu cinema. [4] Referred to in the media as the "Rebel Star", he is one of the highest-paid actors in Indian cinema.[5] Prabhas has been featuring in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list since 2015 [6][7][8] and has received seven Filmfare Awards , a Nandi Award, and a SIIMA Award. Prabhas made his acting debut with the 2002 drama Eeswar,and later attained his breakthrough with the action romance Varsham (2004).
